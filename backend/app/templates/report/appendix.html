Methodology

Nexus Determination

Economic nexus is determined based on each state's revenue and/or transaction thresholds. Physical nexus is determined based on reported physical presence (employees, property, etc.) in each state.

Liability Estimation

Estimated tax liability is calculated using each state's combined state and average local tax rates applied to taxable sales. Interest and penalties are calculated from the estimated nexus date using state-specific rates and methods.

VDA Savings Calculation

VDA (Voluntary Disclosure Agreement) savings are estimated based on typical penalty waivers offered by each state. Most states waive penalties entirely through VDA programs; some also offer reduced or waived interest.
